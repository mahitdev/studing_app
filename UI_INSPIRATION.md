# UI Inspiration Notes

This redesign adapts proven productivity UX patterns from:

- Forest: focus discipline and consequence framing
  - https://www.forestapp.cc/
- Todoist Karma: streak pressure, goals, and progress visualizations
  - https://www.todoist.com/karma
- Rize Productivity: focus pulse concepts and session quality feedback
  - https://rize.io/features/productivity
  - https://rize.io/features/automatic-time-tracking
- RescueTime: weekly performance framing and productivity reporting
  - https://help.rescuetime.com/article/36-personal-productivity

## What was adapted
- Command-center style dashboard with high-signal metrics first.
- Streak and punishment status surfaced in persistent top-level pills.
- Trend chart for 14-day goal completion consistency.
- Focus Pulse score to summarize session quality and distraction impact.
- Brutal accountability messaging with compliance rate and wasted-hours language.
